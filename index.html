Common Questions

Questions we hear all the time.

Do I own the website if I cancel?
+
Yes. The website is yours. If you ever leave, we hand over every file, every page, and every login. We don’t hold your site hostage — we earn your monthly fee by actually delivering results.
How is this different from Wix or a $500 website?
+
A Wix site sits there. Ours is built to rank in Google search for your trade and your cities — and it keeps growing every month with new pages. Plus it has the missed-call text-back and review automation built in. A $500 website is a brochure. This is a lead machine.
What if I already have a website?
+
We can add missed-call text-back and review automation to your existing site — that’s the Maintenance or Lead Catcher plan with no build fee. Or if your current site isn’t ranking or converting, we build you a better one. Text us and we’ll tell you honestly which makes more sense.
How do I know it’s actually getting me calls?
+
Every call and text that comes through your site is tracked. You get a monthly plain-English report showing exactly how many contacts came in, which pages they found you on, and what they searched. No guessing.
Do you work outside Tampa?
+
Yes — we build for trade businesses all across Florida. Orlando, Jacksonville, Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Sarasota, Cape Coral, and everywhere in between. We focus on Florida only so we actually know the markets.
How long until my site is live?
+
Two weeks from the day you approve the preview. We build fast because we don’t stack up a backlog — I take 4 sites a month so each one gets the full attention it needs.
I already get plenty of work from word of mouth. Why do I need a website?
+
Word of mouth is great — until someone asks a friend, gets your name, Googles you, and finds nothing. They call the next guy instead. You also can’t answer every call when you’re on a job. Word of mouth brings them to the door; the missed-call text-back and your website are what convert them when you’re not available to answer.
What if I don’t have photos of my work?
+
No problem — we include professional stock photos matched to your trade. We keep a library of high-quality roofing, plumbing, HVAC, and other trade photos that look great and are free to use. If you do have real job photos on your phone or Google Business Profile, send them over and we’ll use those instead. Either way, your site looks professional on day one.
Is this a contract? What am I signing?
+
No contract. The build fee is one-time — that’s yours. The monthly plan is month-to-month and you can cancel anytime by texting me. No 12-month agency lock-in, no cancellation fee. The only thing you sign is a simple service agreement so we both know what’s included. Note: bundle packages (where the build is discounted) have a 3- or 6-month minimum — that’s what locks the discount. After the minimum, it’s month-to-month with no penalty.
Do I have to be on my phone or computer for any of this?
+
No. Everything runs in the background. Missed-call texts fire the moment you don’t pick up. Review requests go out automatically after a job. Monthly ranking pages go live without you touching anything. If we ever need something from you — a quick approval, a photo, an answer — we text you. You reply when you have a minute. That’s the whole job on your end.
What’s the catch with the 90-day guarantee?
+
No catch. If your site doesn’t generate at least one verifiable new customer inquiry in 90 days, we refund one month’s management fee. “New customer” means a call, text, or form submission tracked through your dedicated call tracking number — we can prove every contact. No fine print.
